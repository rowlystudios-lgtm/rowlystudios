Rowly Studios — Contractor Agreement READ IN FULL

Introduction

This agreement is between Rowly Studios ("the Agency") and you ("the Contractor"). It is written in plain language. Our goal is a partnership that works for both of us.

1. What Rowly Studios Provides

As a Rowly Studios Approved contractor you receive: consistent client job placement; a guaranteed rate floor on every RS-sourced job (starting at $450/day and up, confirmed at onboarding); a profile on the RS talent roster with regular social media spotlight features; and professional infrastructure including client communication, invoicing support, and brief management handled by the Agency.

2. Rates & Payment

Your agreed minimum day rate will be confirmed in your approval email. RS applies a placement markup to the client rate. Your agreed day rate is what you receive — the markup is the Agency fee and is never deducted from your payment.

RS pays contractors within 14 days of invoice approval, regardless of when the client pays RS. You invoice RS only — never the client directly.

Rates are reviewed by mutual agreement every 6 months.

3. Your Existing Clients — Fully Protected

Your existing client relationships are entirely and unconditionally yours. This agreement does not affect them in any way. You may continue to work with any client you currently have, independently and without any obligation to the Agency. This cannot be changed.

If you choose to bring an existing client under the Rowly Studios umbrella to access insurance or production infrastructure, a reduced referral rate applies. This is always optional and agreed in advance.

4. Non-Solicitation

This clause applies only to clients that Rowly Studios introduces you to — it does not apply to your existing clients under any circumstances whatsoever.

For 12 months following the completion of a Rowly-sourced project, you agree not to work directly with that specific client outside of the Agency without prior written consent from RS. If a Rowly-sourced client approaches you directly, you agree to notify the Agency within 7 days.

This is not a non-compete. You are free to work with any other agency, platform, or client independently at any time.

5. Intellectual Property

IP terms are agreed per project in the brief. Default: the client owns final deliverables upon full payment. The contractor retains no rights to deliverables from RS-placed projects unless separately negotiated. Behind-the-scenes content may be used in your portfolio with Agency approval.

6. Conduct & Brand Standards

On RS engagements you represent the Agency. You agree to: conduct yourself professionally in all client interactions; not use the RS name or logo in personal marketing without written approval; credit RS-placed work as "via Rowly Studios" unless otherwise agreed; and notify the Agency immediately if any on-set issue arises.

7. Social Media & Spotlight Rights

By submitting your demo reel and joining the network, you grant Rowly Studios the right to use your submitted footage and profile information to create and publish branded spotlight content across RS social channels. You will be tagged and credited in all posts and may reshare this content to your own channels.

8. Confidentiality

You agree to keep client information, project details, and RS rate structures confidential during and after your time in the network.

9. Term & Termination

This agreement begins on the date of your approval and continues until terminated by either party with 30 days written notice. Either party may terminate immediately in the event of a material breach. The non-solicitation clause (Section 4) survives termination for 12 months from the last completed RS project.

10. Independent Contractor Status

You are an independent contractor, not an employee of Rowly Studios. You are responsible for your own taxes and compliance. RS provides set and kit liability coverage on RS-placed jobs only.
