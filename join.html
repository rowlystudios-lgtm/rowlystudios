Rowly Studios — Freelance Contractor Agreement

This agreement is entered into between Rowly Studios ("the Company") and the applicant ("the Contractor") upon submission of this application and confirmation of membership.

1. Independent Contractor Status

The Contractor agrees they are an independent freelance contractor, not an employee of Rowly Studios. The Contractor is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and business expenses.

2. Platform Usage

The Contractor agrees to conduct all bookings arranged through the Rowly Studios platform exclusively through the platform. The Contractor agrees not to directly solicit, accept, or arrange work with any client first introduced through the Rowly Studios platform outside of the platform, for a period of 12 months from the date of first introduction.

3. Non-Solicitation / Non-Circumvention

The Contractor agrees not to directly contact or accept direct bookings from clients they were introduced to through Rowly Studios, outside of the Rowly Studios platform, for a period of 12 months following introduction. Breach of this clause may result in removal from the platform and the Company reserving the right to seek compensation for lost platform fees.

4. Rates

The Contractor sets their own day rate and half-day rate. All rates are subject to a platform minimum floor. Rates agreed at the time of booking are final and may not be renegotiated after a booking is confirmed.

5. Conduct

The Contractor agrees to maintain professional conduct on all bookings, communicate promptly through the platform, and uphold the standards expected of a Rowly Studios verified creative.

6. Platform Fees

Rowly Studios charges a platform service fee on bookings facilitated through the platform. Fee details are disclosed in the app at the time of booking.

7. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California.

By submitting this application, the Contractor agrees to the above terms and conditions.

Client Platform Agreement

Rowly Studios — Client Platform Agreement

This agreement is between Rowly Studios ("the Company") and the registering company or individual ("the Client") upon registration.

1. Platform Use

The Client agrees to use the Rowly Studios platform exclusively for booking talent that has been introduced through the platform. The Client agrees not to directly book or engage any talent member first introduced through Rowly Studios outside of the platform, for a period of 12 months from the date of first introduction.

2. Non-Circumvention

Directly engaging Rowly Studios talent outside the platform constitutes a breach of this agreement. The Company reserves the right to invoice the Client for the equivalent platform service fee for any off-platform work arranged with introduced talent.

3. Payment Terms

All jobs posted through the platform are subject to Rowly Studios standard payment terms. Invoices are due within 14 days. Late payment fees apply as set out in the Rowly Studios Payment Terms Policy.

4. Confidentiality

The Client agrees to keep the rates, availability, and contact details of Rowly Studios talent confidential and not to share this information with third parties.

5. Conduct

The Client agrees to treat all talent with respect and professionalism. Harassment, discrimination, or misconduct will result in immediate removal from the platform.

6. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California.

By registering, the Client agrees to the above terms and conditions.
